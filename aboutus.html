About MyGroup

MyGroup is a grassroots activism club built on the belief that ordinary people, working together, can create extraordinary change.

We are volunteer-led, community-rooted, and action-oriented. Our work spans education, outreach, advocacy, and mutual aid, always guided by principles of solidarity, respect, and accountability.

Our Values

  • Community-led decision making
  • Nonviolent, constructive action
  • Inclusivity and mutual respect
  • Transparency and shared leadership

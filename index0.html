Grassroots Action. Real Change.

MyGroup is a community-driven activism club working for a more just and compassionate world.

Our Initiatives

Community Outreach

We organize local events, canvassing efforts, and volunteer programs that build relationships and empower people to take action in their own neighborhoods.

Education & Awareness

Through workshops, teach-ins, and online content, we help people understand the issues and develop the skills needed for effective activism.

Policy Advocacy

We engage with decision-makers, support grassroots campaigns, and advocate for policies that reflect our shared values.

Mutual Aid

We coordinate mutual aid projects that directly support people in need through solidarity, not charity.

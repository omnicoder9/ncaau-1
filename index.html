North Carolina Animal Advocates United

We are a grassroots, loosely organized group of activists interested in furthering animal rights in North Carolina. By "animal rights" we mean anything that reduces the suffering and exploitation of all non-human animals. While we support animal liberation in its entirety, we understand and support welfare initiatives that help meet our goal. As a collective that's loosely organized, we can align ourselves with any animal rights work orchestrated by another organization or individual. We are also empowered to pursue our own actions and activism as we see fit. All campaigns and actions are encouraged so long as they adhere to our core values and code of conduct.
